📚 Favourite Manhwa

“On The Way To Meet Mom” (엄마를 만나러 가는 길) by GOMYANG

One day, after being discovered in an abandoned house by a rescue team, Mori suddenly finds himself in a children's protection center. Here, he begins to learn a new way of life – one that includes grand care packages sent by a mysterious patron...

💬 My opinion: This is my favourite manhwa! I absolutely love all the characters and the magical, emotional theme of the world. The art and rendering are so fascinating, and baby Mori is the cutest ever 🍼! 10/10 for the plot, world setting, and character design. Highly recommended! 💗

🎬 Favourite Movie

✨ One of my all-time favourite movies is Howl's Moving Castle, a magical anime directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. The story follows Sophie, a young woman who is cursed by a witch and turned into an old lady. She finds refuge in a mysterious moving castle owned by the wizard Howl. 🏰💫

This movie is filled with beautiful animation, heartwarming characters, and a breathtaking soundtrack that stays with you forever. I especially love the fantasy elements, Howl’s transformations, and the bond between the characters. It’s truly a masterpiece that I never get tired of watching! 🎶💖
